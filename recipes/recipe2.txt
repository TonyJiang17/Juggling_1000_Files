Experimental pie number 2!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
5 teaspoons of pecans
3 grams of peaches
5 cups of vanilla
4 kilograms of nutmeg

For the crust:
3 tablespoons of sugar
5 teaspoons of shortening

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Chop the pecans.
2. Heat up the peaches.
3. Combine the nutmeg and the vanilla.

For the crust:
1. Stir in the sugar.
2. Chop the shortening.

Bake at 450 degrees for 30 minutes
