Experimental pie number 18!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
3 teaspoons of chicken
6 grams of beef
9 pounds of garlic
8 ounces of poblanos
5 cups of onions

For the crust:
5 teaspoons of sugar

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Simmer the chicken on low heat.
2. Chop the garlic.
3. Combine the beef and the poblanos.
4. Chop the onions.

For the crust:
1. Stir in the sugar.

Bake at 450 degrees for 30 minutes
