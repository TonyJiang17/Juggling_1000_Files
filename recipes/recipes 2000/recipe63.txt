Experimental pie number 63!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
4 kilograms of blueberries
10 tablespoons of cinnamon

For the crust:
3 tablespoons of flour
5 tablespoons of sugar
4 cups of butter
5 cups of salt
4 tablespoons of shortening

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Combine the blueberries and the cinnamon.

For the crust:
1. Chop the sugar.
2. Mix the flour and the salt together.
3. Mix the shortening and the butter together.

Bake at 450 degrees for 90 minutes
