Experimental pie number 53!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
4 tablespoons of beef
4 tablespoons of tofu
9 grams of chicken
7 teaspoons of pork
10 ounces of parsley

For the crust:
5 teaspoons of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Combine the tofu and the parsley.
2. Mash up the pork.
3. Heat up the chicken.
4. Add the beef.

For the crust:
1. Mash up the butter.

Bake at 250 degrees for 60 minutes
