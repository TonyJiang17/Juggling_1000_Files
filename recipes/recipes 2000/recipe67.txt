Experimental pie number 67!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
10 ounces of apples
5 teaspoons of cherries
5 kilograms of peaches
2 pounds of pecans
3 tablespoons of vanilla

For the crust:
5 cups of shortening
3 teaspoons of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Combine the peaches and the pecans.
2. Mix the apples and the cherries together.
3. Stir in the vanilla.

For the crust:
1. Mix the butter and the shortening together.

Bake at 450 degrees for 40 minutes
