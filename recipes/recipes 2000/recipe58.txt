Experimental pie number 58!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
4 pounds of apples
9 teaspoons of cherries
5 grams of peaches
6 teaspoons of blueberries
8 tablespoons of pecans

For the crust:
3 cups of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the cherries and the peaches together.
2. Stir in the apples.
3. Stir in the pecans.
4. Add the blueberries.

For the crust:
1. Mash up the flour.

Bake at 400 degrees for 50 minutes
