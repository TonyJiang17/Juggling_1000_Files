Experimental pie number 60!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
5 cups of peaches
9 tablespoons of vanilla
10 tablespoons of cinnamon

For the crust:
5 tablespoons of shortening

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mash up the vanilla.
2. Mash up the peaches.
3. Stir in the cinnamon.

For the crust:
1. Heat up the shortening.

Bake at 200 degrees for 60 minutes
