Experimental pie number 57!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
8 ounces of apples
5 kilograms of vanilla
4 cups of cinnamon

For the crust:
5 tablespoons of flour
4 tablespoons of shortening
2 cups of salt

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Add the apples.
2. Combine the vanilla and the cinnamon.

For the crust:
1. Stir in the flour.
2. Stir in the salt.
3. Add the shortening.

Bake at 450 degrees for 40 minutes
