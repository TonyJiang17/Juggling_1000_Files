Experimental pie number 70!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
6 cups of apples
7 cups of nutmeg

For the crust:
4 tablespoons of shortening
3 tablespoons of sugar

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mash up the nutmeg.
2. Heat up the apples.

For the crust:
1. Add the sugar.
2. Chop the shortening.

Bake at 200 degrees for 20 minutes
