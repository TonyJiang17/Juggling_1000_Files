Experimental pie number 65!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
5 teaspoons of pork
10 ounces of cumin
9 pounds of garlic
3 pounds of bell peppers
4 kilograms of poblanos

For the crust:
4 tablespoons of salt

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Add the bell peppers.
2. Combine the poblanos and the garlic.
3. Combine the pork and the cumin.

For the crust:
1. Heat up the salt.

Bake at 200 degrees for 30 minutes
