Experimental pie number 61!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
6 ounces of nutmeg
3 ounces of lemon juice
5 ounces of vanilla

For the crust:
2 tablespoons of salt
2 cups of sugar
5 cups of flour
2 tablespoons of shortening

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Add the vanilla.
2. Combine the lemon juice and the nutmeg.

For the crust:
1. Combine the sugar and the flour.
2. Heat up the salt.
3. Stir in the shortening.

Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes
