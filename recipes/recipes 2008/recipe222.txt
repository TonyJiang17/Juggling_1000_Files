Experimental pie number 222!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
5 grams of blueberries
4 tablespoons of apples
4 pounds of pecans
5 pounds of peaches
3 cups of cherries

For the crust:
3 cups of sugar
3 cups of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the cherries and the blueberries together.
2. Stir in the pecans.
3. Heat up the apples.
4. Mash up the peaches.

For the crust:
1. Heat up the sugar.
2. Heat up the flour.

Bake at 450 degrees for 70 minutes
