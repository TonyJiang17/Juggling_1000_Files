Experimental pie number 213!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
6 cups of chili powder
6 ounces of bell peppers

For the crust:
4 cups of shortening
3 tablespoons of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Simmer the bell peppers on low heat.
2. Chop the chili powder.

For the crust:
1. Mix the butter and the shortening together.

Bake at 250 degrees for 20 minutes
