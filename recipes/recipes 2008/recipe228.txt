Experimental pie number 228!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
7 grams of apples
9 tablespoons of nutmeg
8 pounds of cinnamon
10 grams of vanilla

For the crust:
4 cups of salt
3 tablespoons of shortening

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Combine the nutmeg and the apples.
2. Combine the vanilla and the cinnamon.

For the crust:
1. Simmer the shortening on low heat.
2. Simmer the salt on low heat.

Bake at 400 degrees for 70 minutes
