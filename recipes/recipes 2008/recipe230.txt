Experimental pie number 230!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
4 grams of vanilla
8 grams of cinnamon
3 teaspoons of nutmeg

For the crust:
4 teaspoons of butter
3 cups of shortening
4 teaspoons of sugar
4 tablespoons of salt

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Add the vanilla.
2. Mix the nutmeg and the cinnamon together.

For the crust:
1. Mash up the salt.
2. Chop the butter.
3. Mix the sugar and the shortening together.

Bake at 200 degrees for 90 minutes
