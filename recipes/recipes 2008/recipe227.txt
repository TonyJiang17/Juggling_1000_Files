Experimental pie number 227!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
9 tablespoons of apples
3 pounds of vanilla

For the crust:
2 teaspoons of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Chop the vanilla.
2. Chop the apples.

For the crust:
1. Simmer the flour on low heat.

Bake at 250 degrees for 70 minutes
