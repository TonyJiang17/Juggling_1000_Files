Experimental pie number 211!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
2 tablespoons of cinnamon
6 tablespoons of nutmeg

For the crust:
4 tablespoons of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Combine the cinnamon and the nutmeg.

For the crust:
1. Heat up the butter.

Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes
