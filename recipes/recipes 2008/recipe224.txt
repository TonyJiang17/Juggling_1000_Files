Experimental pie number 224!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
6 kilograms of bell peppers

For the crust:
3 teaspoons of butter
3 tablespoons of salt
3 teaspoons of flour
2 cups of sugar
3 tablespoons of shortening

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Simmer the bell peppers on low heat.

For the crust:
1. Combine the shortening and the salt.
2. Chop the flour.
3. Combine the sugar and the butter.

Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes
