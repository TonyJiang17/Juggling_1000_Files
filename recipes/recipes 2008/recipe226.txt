Experimental pie number 226!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
7 teaspoons of chicken
7 cups of tofu

For the crust:
3 cups of salt
4 tablespoons of flour
5 cups of butter
3 tablespoons of shortening

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Add the tofu.
2. Simmer the chicken on low heat.

For the crust:
1. Combine the salt and the flour.
2. Combine the shortening and the butter.

Bake at 350 degrees for 80 minutes
