Experimental pie number 217!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
3 ounces of blueberries
3 cups of cinnamon

For the crust:
4 teaspoons of flour
2 cups of sugar
2 cups of salt

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mash up the blueberries.
2. Add the cinnamon.

For the crust:
1. Mix the flour and the salt together.
2. Add the sugar.

Bake at 250 degrees for 50 minutes
