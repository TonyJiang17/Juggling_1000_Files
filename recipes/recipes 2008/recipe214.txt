Experimental pie number 214!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
9 teaspoons of peaches
2 teaspoons of lemon juice

For the crust:
5 tablespoons of flour
4 teaspoons of shortening
2 cups of butter
4 tablespoons of salt
4 teaspoons of sugar

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Heat up the lemon juice.
2. Mash up the peaches.

For the crust:
1. Heat up the flour.
2. Mash up the shortening.
3. Combine the salt and the sugar.
4. Add the butter.

Bake at 200 degrees for 70 minutes
