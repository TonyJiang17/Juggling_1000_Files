Experimental pie number 219!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
9 tablespoons of chicken
2 cups of onions

For the crust:
4 cups of butter
2 teaspoons of flour
2 cups of shortening
3 teaspoons of sugar
5 tablespoons of salt

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Simmer the chicken on low heat.
2. Add the onions.

For the crust:
1. Combine the sugar and the shortening.
2. Mix the flour and the butter together.
3. Simmer the salt on low heat.

Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes
