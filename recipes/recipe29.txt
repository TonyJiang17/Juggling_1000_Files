Experimental pie number 29!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
2 pounds of nutmeg
5 ounces of vanilla

For the crust:
4 cups of shortening
4 cups of flour
3 tablespoons of sugar
5 cups of butter
3 teaspoons of salt

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the vanilla and the nutmeg together.

For the crust:
1. Add the butter.
2. Mix the salt and the sugar together.
3. Mix the flour and the shortening together.

Bake at 400 degrees for 90 minutes
