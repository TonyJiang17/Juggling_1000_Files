Experimental pie number 135!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
6 grams of pecans

For the crust:
3 teaspoons of flour
3 cups of salt
3 teaspoons of butter
3 cups of shortening

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Stir in the pecans.

For the crust:
1. Combine the shortening and the salt.
2. Simmer the flour on low heat.
3. Heat up the butter.

Bake at 200 degrees for 60 minutes
