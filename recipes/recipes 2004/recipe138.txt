Experimental pie number 138!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
2 kilograms of cherries
5 kilograms of pecans
7 kilograms of apples

For the crust:
2 teaspoons of butter
2 cups of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Stir in the pecans.
2. Add the apples.
3. Chop the cherries.

For the crust:
1. Mix the flour and the butter together.

Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes
