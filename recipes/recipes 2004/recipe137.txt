Experimental pie number 137!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
6 pounds of cinnamon

For the crust:
5 teaspoons of shortening
3 teaspoons of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Heat up the cinnamon.

For the crust:
1. Mix the shortening and the butter together.

Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes
