Experimental pie number 136!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
10 teaspoons of cherries
10 kilograms of peaches

For the crust:
2 cups of salt

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the peaches and the cherries together.

For the crust:
1. Heat up the salt.

Bake at 200 degrees for 50 minutes
