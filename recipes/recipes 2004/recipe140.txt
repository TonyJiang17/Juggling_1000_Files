Experimental pie number 140!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
7 tablespoons of cinnamon
3 pounds of nutmeg

For the crust:
4 teaspoons of sugar
3 tablespoons of flour
3 tablespoons of shortening
5 teaspoons of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Simmer the nutmeg on low heat.
2. Simmer the cinnamon on low heat.

For the crust:
1. Add the sugar.
2. Mash up the butter.
3. Mix the flour and the shortening together.

Bake at 350 degrees for 60 minutes
