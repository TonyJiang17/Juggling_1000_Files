Experimental pie number 149!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
3 tablespoons of apples
10 grams of pecans
7 teaspoons of peaches

For the crust:
2 teaspoons of sugar

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Combine the pecans and the peaches.
2. Mash up the apples.

For the crust:
1. Heat up the sugar.

Bake at 450 degrees for 40 minutes
