Experimental pie number 4!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
10 kilograms of cherries
10 tablespoons of apples
2 tablespoons of blueberries
4 teaspoons of peaches
10 tablespoons of pecans

For the crust:
5 tablespoons of shortening

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the cherries and the pecans together.
2. Combine the peaches and the apples.
3. Simmer the blueberries on low heat.

For the crust:
1. Chop the shortening.

Bake at 250 degrees for 60 minutes
