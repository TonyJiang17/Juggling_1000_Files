Experimental pie number 102!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
5 pounds of garlic
6 tablespoons of chili powder
4 teaspoons of parsley
8 pounds of poblanos

For the crust:
2 tablespoons of butter
2 cups of salt
5 cups of sugar

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mash up the garlic.
2. Combine the parsley and the poblanos.
3. Add the chili powder.

For the crust:
1. Heat up the butter.
2. Heat up the salt.
3. Simmer the sugar on low heat.

Bake at 350 degrees for 90 minutes
