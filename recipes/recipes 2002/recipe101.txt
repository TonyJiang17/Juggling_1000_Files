Experimental pie number 101!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
10 tablespoons of nutmeg
4 tablespoons of cinnamon
8 cups of lemon juice

For the crust:
4 cups of flour
3 tablespoons of salt
4 teaspoons of shortening
5 cups of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Chop the nutmeg.
2. Chop the lemon juice.
3. Add the cinnamon.

For the crust:
1. Add the flour.
2. Mix the shortening and the salt together.
3. Add the butter.

Bake at 400 degrees for 90 minutes
