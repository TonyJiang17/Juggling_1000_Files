Experimental pie number 95!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
4 pounds of peaches
10 tablespoons of cherries
4 grams of cinnamon
2 cups of nutmeg

For the crust:
2 teaspoons of shortening
4 teaspoons of sugar

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Combine the nutmeg and the peaches.
2. Mix the cinnamon and the cherries together.

For the crust:
1. Chop the sugar.
2. Add the shortening.

Bake at 250 degrees for 90 minutes
