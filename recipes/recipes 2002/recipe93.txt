Experimental pie number 93!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
6 grams of apples
2 cups of peaches
9 tablespoons of nutmeg

For the crust:
3 tablespoons of flour
2 cups of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Combine the nutmeg and the peaches.
2. Stir in the apples.

For the crust:
1. Mix the butter and the flour together.

Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes
