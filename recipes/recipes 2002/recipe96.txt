Experimental pie number 96!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
9 ounces of cinnamon
10 tablespoons of nutmeg

For the crust:
2 tablespoons of flour
3 tablespoons of butter
4 tablespoons of shortening
3 tablespoons of sugar
4 cups of salt

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the cinnamon and the nutmeg together.

For the crust:
1. Combine the sugar and the butter.
2. Combine the salt and the flour.
3. Chop the shortening.

Bake at 250 degrees for 70 minutes
