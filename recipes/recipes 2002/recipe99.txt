Experimental pie number 99!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
4 pounds of apples
9 kilograms of cherries
9 tablespoons of blueberries
2 teaspoons of peaches
2 teaspoons of cinnamon
7 teaspoons of nutmeg

For the crust:
2 teaspoons of sugar

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Combine the blueberries and the cinnamon.
2. Mix the apples and the cherries together.
3. Mash up the peaches.
4. Heat up the nutmeg.

For the crust:
1. Chop the sugar.

Bake at 200 degrees for 40 minutes
