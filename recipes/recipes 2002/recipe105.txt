Experimental pie number 105!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
10 kilograms of cinnamon
7 grams of lemon juice

For the crust:
3 tablespoons of salt

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Combine the lemon juice and the cinnamon.

For the crust:
1. Stir in the salt.

Bake at 300 degrees for 20 minutes
