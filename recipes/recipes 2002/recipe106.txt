Experimental pie number 106!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
6 teaspoons of blueberries
6 teaspoons of nutmeg
4 tablespoons of vanilla
2 ounces of cinnamon
4 tablespoons of lemon juice

For the crust:
3 cups of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Combine the cinnamon and the lemon juice.
2. Stir in the nutmeg.
3. Mix the vanilla and the blueberries together.

For the crust:
1. Chop the butter.

Bake at 400 degrees for 70 minutes
