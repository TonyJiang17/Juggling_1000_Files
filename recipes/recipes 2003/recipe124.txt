Experimental pie number 124!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
2 ounces of cherries
4 tablespoons of pecans
6 ounces of peaches

For the crust:
5 cups of butter
5 cups of salt
4 teaspoons of shortening
3 cups of sugar
3 cups of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Combine the pecans and the cherries.
2. Mash up the peaches.

For the crust:
1. Combine the salt and the flour.
2. Combine the butter and the shortening.
3. Heat up the sugar.

Bake at 200 degrees for 90 minutes
