Experimental pie number 130!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
4 cups of apples
6 cups of nutmeg
2 teaspoons of vanilla

For the crust:
3 teaspoons of sugar

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Simmer the nutmeg on low heat.
2. Add the apples.
3. Heat up the vanilla.

For the crust:
1. Mash up the sugar.

Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes
