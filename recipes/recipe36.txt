Experimental pie number 36!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
10 pounds of chicken
2 grams of cumin
5 teaspoons of chili powder
5 kilograms of garlic
5 kilograms of parsley

For the crust:
5 tablespoons of salt

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the parsley and the garlic together.
2. Mix the chicken and the chili powder together.
3. Chop the cumin.

For the crust:
1. Chop the salt.

Bake at 300 degrees for 40 minutes
