Experimental pie number 26!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
3 teaspoons of lemon juice

For the crust:
5 tablespoons of butter
3 tablespoons of salt
3 cups of shortening
4 teaspoons of sugar

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Simmer the lemon juice on low heat.

For the crust:
1. Heat up the shortening.
2. Combine the salt and the butter.
3. Simmer the sugar on low heat.

Bake at 350 degrees for 80 minutes
