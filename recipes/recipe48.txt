Experimental pie number 48!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
4 cups of pecans
9 cups of blueberries
7 pounds of nutmeg

For the crust:
2 tablespoons of salt

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the blueberries and the nutmeg together.
2. Simmer the pecans on low heat.

For the crust:
1. Mash up the salt.

Bake at 200 degrees for 80 minutes
