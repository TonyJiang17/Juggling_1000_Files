Experimental pie number 31!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
5 kilograms of vanilla
9 teaspoons of lemon juice

For the crust:
5 teaspoons of salt

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the lemon juice and the vanilla together.

For the crust:
1. Add the salt.

Bake at 450 degrees for 90 minutes
