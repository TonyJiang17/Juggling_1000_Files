Experimental pie number 25!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
4 grams of apples
2 teaspoons of blueberries

For the crust:
2 tablespoons of sugar
4 teaspoons of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Combine the blueberries and the apples.

For the crust:
1. Simmer the flour on low heat.
2. Stir in the sugar.

Bake at 250 degrees for 50 minutes
