Experimental pie number 30!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
8 cups of peaches
9 tablespoons of blueberries
5 pounds of cinnamon

For the crust:
4 teaspoons of sugar

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Add the blueberries.
2. Combine the cinnamon and the peaches.

For the crust:
1. Stir in the sugar.

Bake at 300 degrees for 50 minutes
