Experimental pie number 40!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
6 kilograms of blueberries
3 tablespoons of cherries
2 tablespoons of apples

For the crust:
4 tablespoons of sugar

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Heat up the apples.
2. Combine the cherries and the blueberries.

For the crust:
1. Mash up the sugar.

Bake at 400 degrees for 40 minutes
