Experimental pie number 79!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
8 kilograms of cherries
5 cups of pecans
7 pounds of lemon juice
9 ounces of nutmeg

For the crust:
3 tablespoons of salt
2 cups of shortening
5 tablespoons of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mash up the lemon juice.
2. Combine the nutmeg and the pecans.
3. Mash up the cherries.

For the crust:
1. Add the shortening.
2. Add the butter.
3. Chop the salt.

Bake at 400 degrees for 90 minutes
