Experimental pie number 89!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
6 teaspoons of pecans
7 kilograms of lemon juice
8 ounces of nutmeg
3 kilograms of vanilla

For the crust:
3 teaspoons of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Combine the nutmeg and the vanilla.
2. Chop the pecans.
3. Chop the lemon juice.

For the crust:
1. Stir in the butter.

Bake at 250 degrees for 70 minutes
