Experimental pie number 72!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
7 grams of peaches
3 teaspoons of cherries
10 pounds of apples
4 ounces of lemon juice
10 cups of cinnamon
7 ounces of nutmeg

For the crust:
3 teaspoons of sugar

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mash up the nutmeg.
2. Stir in the cinnamon.
3. Combine the cherries and the peaches.
4. Chop the apples.
5. Stir in the lemon juice.

For the crust:
1. Stir in the sugar.

Bake at 300 degrees for 50 minutes
