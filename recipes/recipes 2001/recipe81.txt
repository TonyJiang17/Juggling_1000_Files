Experimental pie number 81!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
8 cups of bell peppers
7 teaspoons of peas
3 teaspoons of poblanos

For the crust:
2 tablespoons of shortening
2 cups of flour
5 teaspoons of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mash up the peas.
2. Combine the bell peppers and the poblanos.

For the crust:
1. Mix the flour and the shortening together.
2. Chop the butter.

Bake at 450 degrees for 40 minutes
