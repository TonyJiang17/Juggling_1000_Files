Experimental pie number 85!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
3 tablespoons of cumin
6 ounces of poblanos

For the crust:
4 teaspoons of salt
5 cups of flour
3 tablespoons of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Add the poblanos.
2. Simmer the cumin on low heat.

For the crust:
1. Chop the butter.
2. Mix the flour and the salt together.

Bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes
