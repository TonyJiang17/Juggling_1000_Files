Experimental pie number 86!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
6 teaspoons of peaches
8 grams of nutmeg
4 kilograms of cinnamon
2 kilograms of lemon juice

For the crust:
3 cups of butter
3 cups of flour
2 teaspoons of shortening

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the nutmeg and the lemon juice together.
2. Mix the peaches and the cinnamon together.

For the crust:
1. Heat up the shortening.
2. Heat up the flour.
3. Stir in the butter.

Bake at 250 degrees for 40 minutes
