Experimental pie number 233!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
8 ounces of apples
5 cups of pecans

For the crust:
3 tablespoons of shortening
2 tablespoons of sugar
4 tablespoons of butter
5 tablespoons of flour
3 tablespoons of salt

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the apples and the pecans together.

For the crust:
1. Combine the butter and the sugar.
2. Mix the flour and the salt together.
3. Simmer the shortening on low heat.

Bake at 250 degrees for 50 minutes
