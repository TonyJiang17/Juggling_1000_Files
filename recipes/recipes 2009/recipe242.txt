Experimental pie number 242!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
2 ounces of garlic
10 tablespoons of parsley
9 ounces of poblanos
8 kilograms of peas

For the crust:
2 teaspoons of butter
2 cups of salt

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the parsley and the peas together.
2. Simmer the poblanos on low heat.
3. Simmer the garlic on low heat.

For the crust:
1. Chop the butter.
2. Simmer the salt on low heat.

Bake at 450 degrees for 40 minutes
