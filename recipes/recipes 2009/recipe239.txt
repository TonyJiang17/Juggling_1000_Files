Experimental pie number 239!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
8 teaspoons of peaches
2 cups of cherries
2 ounces of cinnamon

For the crust:
5 cups of butter
4 tablespoons of shortening
3 cups of flour
2 tablespoons of salt

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Simmer the peaches on low heat.
2. Simmer the cinnamon on low heat.
3. Heat up the cherries.

For the crust:
1. Mix the shortening and the flour together.
2. Mix the butter and the salt together.

Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes
