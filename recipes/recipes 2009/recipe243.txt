Experimental pie number 243!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
4 cups of pecans
7 teaspoons of apples
5 cups of cherries
7 ounces of cinnamon

For the crust:
3 tablespoons of salt
3 tablespoons of butter
2 tablespoons of sugar

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Simmer the cinnamon on low heat.
2. Mash up the cherries.
3. Add the apples.
4. Simmer the pecans on low heat.

For the crust:
1. Combine the sugar and the butter.
2. Add the salt.

Bake at 450 degrees for 90 minutes
