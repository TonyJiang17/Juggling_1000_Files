Experimental pie number 236!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
7 ounces of chicken
5 ounces of tofu
8 pounds of poblanos

For the crust:
2 tablespoons of sugar
5 cups of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Chop the poblanos.
2. Add the chicken.
3. Mash up the tofu.

For the crust:
1. Mash up the sugar.
2. Chop the butter.

Bake at 350 degrees for 60 minutes
