Experimental pie number 237!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
2 pounds of beef
2 teaspoons of tofu
10 teaspoons of carrots
6 ounces of bell peppers

For the crust:
2 tablespoons of sugar

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Combine the tofu and the bell peppers.
2. Combine the beef and the carrots.

For the crust:
1. Add the sugar.

Bake at 250 degrees for 20 minutes
