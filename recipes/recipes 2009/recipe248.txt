Experimental pie number 248!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
7 kilograms of cherries
2 kilograms of peaches
8 ounces of blueberries
2 teaspoons of pecans
2 ounces of apples
10 pounds of nutmeg

For the crust:
2 tablespoons of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Combine the blueberries and the nutmeg.
2. Combine the peaches and the cherries.
3. Stir in the pecans.
4. Mash up the apples.

For the crust:
1. Heat up the butter.

Bake at 200 degrees for 50 minutes
