Experimental pie number 238!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
5 teaspoons of pecans
3 grams of apples
6 teaspoons of peaches
2 kilograms of blueberries

For the crust:
4 cups of butter
5 teaspoons of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Add the peaches.
2. Chop the blueberries.
3. Heat up the apples.
4. Heat up the pecans.

For the crust:
1. Chop the butter.
2. Heat up the flour.

Bake at 400 degrees for 50 minutes
