Experimental pie number 235!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
6 ounces of tofu
4 grams of pork
10 tablespoons of beef
9 grams of garlic
6 tablespoons of carrots

For the crust:
5 cups of butter
5 cups of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Stir in the garlic.
2. Heat up the carrots.
3. Mix the pork and the beef together.
4. Simmer the tofu on low heat.

For the crust:
1. Combine the butter and the flour.

Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes
