Experimental pie number 246!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
2 ounces of cherries
6 kilograms of vanilla

For the crust:
4 teaspoons of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Heat up the cherries.
2. Stir in the vanilla.

For the crust:
1. Chop the butter.

Bake at 250 degrees for 60 minutes
