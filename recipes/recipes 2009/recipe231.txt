Experimental pie number 231!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
7 cups of blueberries
6 tablespoons of pecans
9 tablespoons of lemon juice

For the crust:
4 teaspoons of shortening
2 tablespoons of salt

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Stir in the blueberries.
2. Stir in the lemon juice.
3. Stir in the pecans.

For the crust:
1. Chop the salt.
2. Stir in the shortening.

Bake at 400 degrees for 60 minutes
