Experimental pie number 33!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
6 ounces of apples
4 ounces of nutmeg

For the crust:
5 tablespoons of shortening
2 tablespoons of flour
4 cups of sugar
2 cups of butter
3 tablespoons of salt

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Add the nutmeg.
2. Chop the apples.

For the crust:
1. Simmer the flour on low heat.
2. Stir in the shortening.
3. Heat up the salt.
4. Simmer the butter on low heat.
5. Heat up the sugar.

Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes
