Experimental pie number 162!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
7 kilograms of blueberries
8 teaspoons of cherries
8 cups of apples
3 ounces of peaches
7 pounds of pecans
5 tablespoons of nutmeg

For the crust:
5 cups of sugar

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Stir in the blueberries.
2. Heat up the pecans.
3. Heat up the cherries.
4. Mix the apples and the nutmeg together.
5. Add the peaches.

For the crust:
1. Add the sugar.

Bake at 200 degrees for 20 minutes
