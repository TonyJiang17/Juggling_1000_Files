Experimental pie number 159!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
4 cups of blueberries

For the crust:
4 cups of shortening
5 teaspoons of sugar
2 tablespoons of salt
3 cups of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Simmer the blueberries on low heat.

For the crust:
1. Combine the shortening and the salt.
2. Add the sugar.
3. Add the flour.

Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes
