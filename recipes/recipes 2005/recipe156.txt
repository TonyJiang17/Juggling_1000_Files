Experimental pie number 156!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
7 grams of beef
3 teaspoons of chicken
4 ounces of onions

For the crust:
5 cups of flour
2 cups of salt
2 teaspoons of butter
3 teaspoons of shortening

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the beef and the chicken together.
2. Heat up the onions.

For the crust:
1. Mash up the salt.
2. Stir in the shortening.
3. Mix the butter and the flour together.

Bake at 450 degrees for 90 minutes
