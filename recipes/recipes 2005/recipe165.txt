Experimental pie number 165!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
9 teaspoons of chicken
2 cups of beef
8 cups of pork
2 teaspoons of garlic

For the crust:
3 tablespoons of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the pork and the chicken together.
2. Stir in the garlic.
3. Heat up the beef.

For the crust:
1. Chop the butter.

Bake at 300 degrees for 90 minutes
