Experimental pie number 168!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
9 grams of nutmeg
7 teaspoons of lemon juice

For the crust:
3 cups of salt
2 cups of butter
5 teaspoons of shortening
4 tablespoons of sugar
3 teaspoons of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the lemon juice and the nutmeg together.

For the crust:
1. Heat up the butter.
2. Combine the sugar and the salt.
3. Heat up the shortening.
4. Chop the flour.

Bake at 200 degrees for 30 minutes
