Experimental pie number 166!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
9 grams of chicken
3 cups of tofu
2 cups of parsley

For the crust:
3 teaspoons of flour
3 teaspoons of sugar
4 tablespoons of salt

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the parsley and the tofu together.
2. Simmer the chicken on low heat.

For the crust:
1. Combine the sugar and the salt.
2. Stir in the flour.

Bake at 200 degrees for 30 minutes
