Experimental pie number 153!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
7 tablespoons of vanilla
3 ounces of cinnamon
2 grams of lemon juice

For the crust:
2 teaspoons of flour
5 teaspoons of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the vanilla and the lemon juice together.
2. Chop the cinnamon.

For the crust:
1. Stir in the flour.
2. Heat up the butter.

Bake at 350 degrees for 80 minutes
