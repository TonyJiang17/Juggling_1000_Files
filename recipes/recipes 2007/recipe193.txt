Experimental pie number 193!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
4 pounds of parsley
8 ounces of garlic
6 kilograms of cumin
3 ounces of chili powder

For the crust:
4 cups of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Stir in the chili powder.
2. Mix the garlic and the parsley together.
3. Add the cumin.

For the crust:
1. Stir in the flour.

Bake at 350 degrees for 90 minutes
