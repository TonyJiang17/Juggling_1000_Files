Experimental pie number 208!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
5 teaspoons of nutmeg
10 cups of vanilla

For the crust:
5 cups of salt
4 tablespoons of sugar
2 tablespoons of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Add the vanilla.
2. Mash up the nutmeg.

For the crust:
1. Mix the flour and the sugar together.
2. Add the salt.

Bake at 450 degrees for 50 minutes
