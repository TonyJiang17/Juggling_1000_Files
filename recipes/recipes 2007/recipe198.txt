Experimental pie number 198!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
6 cups of peaches
8 teaspoons of cherries
6 ounces of apples
8 pounds of nutmeg
4 kilograms of cinnamon

For the crust:
5 teaspoons of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Combine the apples and the peaches.
2. Mix the cinnamon and the cherries together.
3. Stir in the nutmeg.

For the crust:
1. Chop the butter.

Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes
