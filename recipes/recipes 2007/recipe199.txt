Experimental pie number 199!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
2 tablespoons of lemon juice
2 ounces of cinnamon
3 ounces of nutmeg

For the crust:
3 tablespoons of butter
5 teaspoons of flour
3 cups of shortening

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mash up the cinnamon.
2. Chop the nutmeg.
3. Chop the lemon juice.

For the crust:
1. Combine the flour and the butter.
2. Add the shortening.

Bake at 200 degrees for 90 minutes
