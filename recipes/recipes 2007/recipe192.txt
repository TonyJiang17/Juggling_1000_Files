Experimental pie number 192!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
9 grams of beef
5 tablespoons of tofu
7 grams of pork
2 cups of chicken

For the crust:
4 tablespoons of sugar
3 tablespoons of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Simmer the chicken on low heat.
2. Heat up the pork.
3. Stir in the tofu.
4. Mash up the beef.

For the crust:
1. Mix the sugar and the butter together.

Bake at 300 degrees for 90 minutes
