Experimental pie number 197!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
3 pounds of cherries
7 ounces of cinnamon
3 ounces of nutmeg
9 teaspoons of lemon juice

For the crust:
4 cups of butter
5 cups of shortening
3 tablespoons of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the cherries and the lemon juice together.
2. Stir in the cinnamon.
3. Chop the nutmeg.

For the crust:
1. Combine the shortening and the flour.
2. Chop the butter.

Bake at 300 degrees for 60 minutes
