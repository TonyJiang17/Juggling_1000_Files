Experimental pie number 5!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
6 kilograms of lemon juice
5 kilograms of vanilla

For the crust:
5 cups of salt
2 cups of sugar
5 tablespoons of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Combine the lemon juice and the vanilla.

For the crust:
1. Mix the salt and the sugar together.
2. Add the butter.

Bake at 200 degrees for 80 minutes
