Experimental pie number 183!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
8 ounces of blueberries
3 ounces of pecans
4 ounces of cinnamon

For the crust:
4 teaspoons of butter
3 tablespoons of sugar
4 teaspoons of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the cinnamon and the blueberries together.
2. Add the pecans.

For the crust:
1. Mix the butter and the sugar together.
2. Heat up the flour.

Bake at 200 degrees for 30 minutes
