Experimental pie number 186!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
2 grams of peaches
9 pounds of cherries
4 grams of vanilla
9 pounds of cinnamon

For the crust:
4 cups of butter
2 tablespoons of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the peaches and the vanilla together.
2. Combine the cherries and the cinnamon.

For the crust:
1. Mix the flour and the butter together.

Bake at 300 degrees for 20 minutes
