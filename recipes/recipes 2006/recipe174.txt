Experimental pie number 174!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
2 teaspoons of pork
10 grams of bell peppers

For the crust:
3 cups of sugar
4 teaspoons of butter
3 cups of shortening

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Add the pork.
2. Chop the bell peppers.

For the crust:
1. Simmer the butter on low heat.
2. Mix the shortening and the sugar together.

Bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes
