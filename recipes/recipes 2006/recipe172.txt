Experimental pie number 172!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
4 pounds of peaches
3 teaspoons of cinnamon

For the crust:
4 tablespoons of shortening
2 cups of sugar

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Heat up the peaches.
2. Mash up the cinnamon.

For the crust:
1. Add the shortening.
2. Chop the sugar.

Bake at 400 degrees for 70 minutes
