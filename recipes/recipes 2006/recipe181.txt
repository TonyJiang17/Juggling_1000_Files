Experimental pie number 181!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
9 teaspoons of apples
8 tablespoons of blueberries
3 kilograms of nutmeg
3 grams of cinnamon

For the crust:
2 cups of sugar
4 teaspoons of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Combine the cinnamon and the blueberries.
2. Heat up the nutmeg.
3. Mash up the apples.

For the crust:
1. Chop the flour.
2. Simmer the sugar on low heat.

Bake at 350 degrees for 90 minutes
