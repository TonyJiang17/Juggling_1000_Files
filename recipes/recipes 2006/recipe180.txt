Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
6 teaspoons of cinnamon
10 cups of lemon juice
3 teaspoons of vanilla

For the crust:
3 tablespoons of sugar
3 tablespoons of shortening
3 cups of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Chop the cinnamon.
2. Simmer the lemon juice on low heat.
3. Stir in the vanilla.

For the crust:
1. Combine the butter and the sugar.
2. Chop the shortening.

Bake at 200 degrees for 70 minutes
