Experimental pie number 184!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
8 kilograms of peaches
7 grams of cherries
10 teaspoons of pecans

For the crust:
5 cups of butter
5 teaspoons of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the cherries and the peaches together.
2. Chop the pecans.

For the crust:
1. Combine the flour and the butter.

Bake at 450 degrees for 60 minutes
