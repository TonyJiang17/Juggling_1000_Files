Experimental pie number 20!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
3 pounds of cinnamon
5 cups of vanilla
4 teaspoons of lemon juice

For the crust:
4 teaspoons of shortening
3 teaspoons of butter
5 cups of salt

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the cinnamon and the lemon juice together.
2. Stir in the vanilla.

For the crust:
1. Combine the shortening and the salt.
2. Simmer the butter on low heat.

Bake at 250 degrees for 90 minutes
