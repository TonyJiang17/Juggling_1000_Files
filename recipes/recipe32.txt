Experimental pie number 32!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
8 grams of pork
6 tablespoons of tofu
3 ounces of chicken
8 cups of beef
7 ounces of parsley

For the crust:
5 tablespoons of shortening

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Heat up the tofu.
2. Simmer the chicken on low heat.
3. Heat up the parsley.
4. Combine the pork and the beef.

For the crust:
1. Add the shortening.

Bake at 200 degrees for 70 minutes
