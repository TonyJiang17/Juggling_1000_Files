Experimental pie number 46!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
3 teaspoons of tofu
2 kilograms of pork

For the crust:
5 tablespoons of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Simmer the pork on low heat.
2. Chop the tofu.

For the crust:
1. Stir in the flour.

Bake at 250 degrees for 60 minutes
