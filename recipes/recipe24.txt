Experimental pie number 24!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
8 kilograms of peaches
7 cups of cinnamon

For the crust:
5 cups of shortening
2 cups of flour
3 cups of salt
2 cups of sugar
3 teaspoons of butter

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mash up the peaches.
2. Add the cinnamon.

For the crust:
1. Mash up the shortening.
2. Mash up the flour.
3. Chop the salt.
4. Heat up the butter.
5. Chop the sugar.

Bake at 250 degrees for 40 minutes
