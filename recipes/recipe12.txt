Experimental pie number 12!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
2 ounces of lemon juice
7 kilograms of cinnamon
2 kilograms of nutmeg

For the crust:
3 tablespoons of salt
3 tablespoons of sugar
4 cups of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the nutmeg and the lemon juice together.
2. Stir in the cinnamon.

For the crust:
1. Combine the sugar and the salt.
2. Heat up the flour.

Bake at 450 degrees for 50 minutes
