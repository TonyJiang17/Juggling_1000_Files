Experimental pie number 41!

Sweet Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
2 teaspoons of pecans
9 cups of blueberries
3 ounces of cherries
3 tablespoons of peaches

For the crust:
2 tablespoons of butter
3 teaspoons of salt
4 tablespoons of sugar

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Chop the cherries.
2. Mix the pecans and the peaches together.
3. Chop the blueberries.

For the crust:
1. Combine the salt and the sugar.
2. Add the butter.

Bake at 250 degrees for 90 minutes
