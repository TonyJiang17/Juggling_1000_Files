Experimental pie number 21!

Savory Pie
Ingredients:
For the filling:
4 ounces of chili powder
8 kilograms of parsley
10 ounces of poblanos

For the crust:
3 cups of flour

Instructions:
For the filling:
1. Mix the poblanos and the chili powder together.
2. Stir in the parsley.

For the crust:
1. Simmer the flour on low heat.

Bake at 450 degrees for 80 minutes
